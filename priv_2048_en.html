2048 THE GAME

Privacy Policy

  • Who is requesting Data

    App: 2048 THE GAME
    Developer: Mulakovich
    Website: https://mulakovich.github.io

  • What Data are We requesting

    This application request access to Google Play Games user’s profile and its installation does not require any special permissions.


  • Why We are requesting Data

    This application uses Google Play Games services for achievements and leaderboards.

    The user will be asked to log in to their Google Play Games profile with the purpose of be able to unlock achievements and show their Display Name in leaderboards.

    The user can log out at any time from the application “Settings” button or Google Play Games interface.

    No data from Google Play Games user’s profile will be stored within the App or shared.


  • Ads

    Ads are displayed in the app via Google’s AdMob network.
